Support Morpheus Development

Morpheus is open source software. We kindly ask You to support it's development by allowing anonymous usage statistics to be send to the developers, indicating Morpheus' adoption in the community. You will help us to acquire funding and to continue improving Morpheus.

Specifically, the number of simulated models, an anonymous user id and your operating system type will be send.

No information about your modelling work will be exposed.
You may revoke your decision anytime through morpheus settings.
